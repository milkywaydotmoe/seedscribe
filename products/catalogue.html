The Leaflink Notebook by Seedscribe!

The product that our team developed involves a sustainable, multifunctional notebook constructed from recycled paper waste, incorporating a digital component aimed at enhancing literacy among children facing educational challenges. The notebook's cover will feature a QR code, granting users access to an online learning platform designed to support blended learning. This platform will cater to various learning needs, providing an interactive and engaging environment for children who may struggle with traditional learning methods. The digital resource will complement the notebook's physical content, allowing children to strengthen their literacy skills in both reading and writing.

Each page of the notebook will be embedded with seeds, making the paper biodegradable and reusable. After the notebookâ€™s pages have been used, they can be planted, contributing to the reduction of waste in the community and promoting environmental sustainability. This approach not only mitigates paper waste but also actively supports a cleaner, greener environment by encouraging the planting of trees or plants from the seeds embedded in the paper.

The LeafLink Notebook aims to foster both academic development and environmental awareness among children. By integrating an eco-friendly element into the learning process, the researchers seek to promote sustainable practices while simultaneously improving literacy skills. Through this initiative, the team strive to create a holistic learning experience that not only addresses educational needs but also instills a sense of responsibility for the environment.
