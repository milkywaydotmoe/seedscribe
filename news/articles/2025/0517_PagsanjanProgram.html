Planting the seeds in Pagsanjan!

An outreach to plant seeds of wonder in the youth!

  • March 17, 2025
  • news, product launch, product teaser

We at Seedscribe successfully hosted an outreach program for the youth at Magdapio, Pagsanjan!

Building on the excitement of our program, the Seedscribe team is proud to have planted seeds of knowledge in the children of Magdapio, We hosted lots of games whilst also introducing them to the Seedscribe worksheets, and teaching them about reading, writing, and math, in a fun and unique way!

Watching learners beam with pride as they completed a tricky problem or proudly read their first short sentence reminded us just how powerful early encouragement can be. We believe that the seed we planted can help them grow into the future of our nation, gaining more than just academic skills, but resilience, creativity, and the belief that they can tackle new challenges.

Our hope is that each young mind we touched will carry this confidence forward, pursuing bigger dreams in school, in their careers, and in their communities.
