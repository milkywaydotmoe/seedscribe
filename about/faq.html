Notebook-related Questions

How are the notebooks made?

Our notebooks are crafted and assembled with care by hand using sustainable, 100% recycled paper and locally sourced seeds.

Do you use any harmful chemicals in the production process?

The notebook making process uses absolutely no harmful chemicals, from start to finish, ensuring a product that is safe to use for kids of all ages!

Are the notebooks biodegradable or compostable?

SeedScribe notebooks are both biodegradable and compostable, so they break down naturally. They have lettuce and celery seeds inside. You can plant them in soil, and they will grow into fresh greens!

Where do you source the materials to make these notebooks?

Our seeds are sourced from local sellers and the paper recycled is sourced from local students in the Sta. Cruz area.

What happens to my notebook at the end of its life?

Once you are done with the notebook, you can seperate the pages and plant them in the soil to grow the seeds.

Learning Material Related Questions

What age group are your learning materials designed for?

SeedScribe learning materials are designed for children aged 3 to 8.They make learning about nature, taking care of the environment, and gardening fun and easy. The activities are simple, enjoyable, and perfect for young explorers!

Do i need to buy a Seedscribe notebook to access the Learning Materials?

You do not need to buy a SeedScribe notebook to access our learning material. We offer access for all users.

What subjects or skills do your materials cover?

We offer writing and reading skills, as well as basic math and science skills for students from Kinder to Grade 2.

Do you offer digital or physical learning materials?

Yes, SeedScribe offers both digital and physical learning materials. The digital material is our website, linked on the notebook’s front cover, while the physical material is the notebook itself.

Can these materials be used at home and in schools?

Yes. Seedscribe encourages the use of its learning materials, no matter the location!

Wholesale / Business Related Questions

Are there any bulk discounts?

Unfortunately, We can’t offer discounts because our product is already affordably priced at 35 pesos each, making it well worth the cost. Offering discounts could lead to losses for our company.

Do you offer partnerships for charities or non-profits?

The company offers partnership opportunities for charities and non-profits, reflecting its commitment to giving back to the community. By collaborating with organizations that focus on education, sustainability, and social causes, the company aims to make a greater impact. Through these partnerships, it seeks to provide eco-friendly learning tools that promote literacy and environmental awareness.

Can i become a Seedscribe reseller?

Unfortunately, we cannot accomodate resellers, due to the hand-made nature of our product. Our product is made by hand by a small team, and we simply cannot provide the stock required to supply both our demand, and demand from resellers.
